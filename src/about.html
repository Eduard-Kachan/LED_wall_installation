About

LED Wall Art is a digital interactive accent art on your wall that can be controlled via your computer. A grid of 30 LEDs that is capable of 16 million colours, attached to a wood board. User can change colours on the Canvas of colours, and see the result on the wall.

Arduino will allows interaction with the moving patterns.
